Driving Licence Application Process

You can submit an application for DL in Pune either online or offline. In this section, we discuss the process involved in both methods.

How to apply for a Driving Licence online in Pune

You can refer to the following steps to submit an application online for a Driving Licence in Pune.

  • Step 1: Visit the Parivahan website to access online RTO services.

  • Step 2: Select “DL Related Services” by navigating to the “Online Services” menu.

  • Step 3: Select the state name (Maharashtra) on the next page and click on “Apply for Learner’s Licence” (LL).

  • Step 4: Fill out the online form, upload the documents and pay the application fees to book a test slot.

  • Step 5: Appear for the LL test on the designated date and receive the Learner’s Licence.

  • Step 6: Now, visit the Parivahan website and select the “Apply for DL” option from the DL services menu.

  • Step 7: Select the option “Holding LL” and fill out the online application for a Driving Licence and upload the relevant documents.

  • Step 8: Next, select the “Appointments” option and book a slot for the driving test after 30 days of the issuance of LL.

  • Step 9: Pay the applicable fees and print/download the acknowledgement receipt for future reference.

How to apply for a Driving Licence offline in Pune

Here are the steps to apply for DL offline in Pune.

  • Step 1: Visit the RTO and obtain the LL application form (Form 2). Alternatively, you can download it through the Parivahan website.

  • Step 2: Fill out the form and compile all the required documents.

  • Step 3: Submit the application form and documents to the RTO, pay the fees, collect the acknowledgement receipt and select a slot for the Learner’s Licence test.

  • Step 4: Appear for the test and collect the LL after test clearance.

  • Step 5: Next, you can pick a slot for the driving test after 30 days.

  • Step 6: Pay the DL and test fee and collect the acknowledgement receipt.

  • Step 7: Appear for the driving test on the designated date at the RTO.

Driving test procedure in Pune

In this section, we highlight the process involved in the driving test for issuing a DL in Pune.

Driving test for four-wheelers

  • A driving test for four-wheelers includes two types of tests: ground and road tests.

  • To clear the ground test, you need to drive the vehicle on the “U” and “T” markings.

  • The road test evaluates your driving skills in real-world road conditions.

  • An RTO inspector conducts and monitors the test.

  • If the RTO inspector is unsatisfied with your driving skills, you must retake the test.

Driving test for two/three-wheelers

  • It includes two types of tests: ground and road tests.

  • To clear the ground test, you need to ride/drive the vehicle on the “8” and “T” markings.

  • The road test evaluates your driving skills with respect to traffic rules and regulations.

  • If you don’t clear the test, you can apply for a retest after 7 days.

Frequently Asked Questions on Aadhar Enrollment Form

Q- Why is the Eligibility?

  • The applicant should be 18 years of age.

  • The DL application should hold a valid Learner’s Licence.

  • The LL should not be older than 6 months from the date of issuance.

  • The applicant should appear for a DL test after 30 days of receiving the LL.

  • The applicant should clear the driving test.

Q- Whar are the documents required

  • Form 1 (Self-declaration of physical fitness)

  • Form 1A (Medical Certificate if the applicant is above 40 years of age or for commercial vehicle DL)

  • Form 2 (DL application form)

  • Valid Learner’s Licence

  • Age and address proof (Aadhaar card, Passport, Voter ID card, birth certificate, etc.)

  • Passport-size photographs

Q- What are the documents required?

Following documents are necessary for getting the Maharashtra birth certificate:

  • Identity proof of the parents for verification
  • Parent’s marriage certificate
  • Letter from the hospital – Proof of birth of the child issued by the hospital where the child is born
  • Parents’ birth certificate or SSC marks sheet

Q- Is a driving test necessary?

A driving test is mandatory to get a new DL in Pune.

Q- Can i apply for DL without learners licence?

You cannot apply for a Driving Licence without a valid LL.

Q- What happens if i do not clear exam in first attempt?

If you fail to clear the driving test on the first attempt, you can apply for a retest after 7 days.

Q- Is there any test to get the Learner’s licence?

Yes, you have to appear for a theory test to be eligible to hold a Learner’s Licence.
