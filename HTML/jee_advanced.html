JEE Advanced Eligibility:

  • To be eligible for JEE Advanced, candidates qualifying the 10+2 with a minimum 75% mark (65% for SC / ST candidates) in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Moreover, they need to qualify for the JEE Main and be among the top 2, 50,000 candidates. Students who fulfil these criteria shall be allowed to appear for JEE Advanced.

  • A candidate can attempt JEE (Advanced) maximum of two times in two consecutive years.

Eligibility for Foreign Candidates / OCI/PIO Candidates:

  • The foreign candidates and OCI.PIO candidates need to complete their education equivalent to 10+2 and need not write the JEE Main exam. Such candidates can apply for JEE Advanced directly. Moreover, foreign / OCI /PIO candidates need to complete other requirements too.

What are the documents required for JEE ADVANCED form?

While filing the JEE ADVANCED enrollment form following documents are required to be mentioned by you. Namely,

For all stuedents:
S No Documents Required
1 Class X certificate if the date of birth is mentioned in it OR birth certificate.
2 Class XII (or equivalent examination) certificate [e.g-for those who appeared first time for this examination in 2023 or 2024 or whose Board declared the Class XII (or equivalent examination) result of academic session 2021-22 on or after September 21, 2022]. If marksheet of Class XII (or equivalent examination) for 2024 is available, then it must be uploaded.
3 If the candidate’s name is not same as in the Class X certificate, gazette notification showing the change of name.
For Candidates seeking admission under the GEN-EWS category:
S No Documents Required
1 GEN-EWS certificate should have been issued on or after April 01 of the current year in consonance with the latest guidelines of the Government of India.
2 If any GEN-EWS candidate fails or is unable to submit the GEN-EWS certificate at the time of online registration, the candidate has to upload a declaration.
For Candidates seeking admission under the OBC-NCL category:
S No Documents Required
1 OBC-NCL certificate should have been issued on or after April 01 of the current year in consonance with the latest guidelines of the Government of India. If any OBC-NCL candidate fails or is unable to submit the OBC-NCL certificate at the time of online registration, the candidate has to upload a declaration.
For Candidates seeking admission under the SC or ST category:
S No Documents Required
1 Caste (for SC) or tribe (for ST) certificate (FORM-SC/ST), as per the latest guidelines of the Government of India. If any SC/ST candidate fails or is unable to submit the SC/ST certificate at the time of online registration, the candidate has to upload a declaration [Declaration in Lieu of SC/ST Certificate] to that effect.
For Candidates seeking admission under the PwD category:
S No Documents Required
1 Physical Disability certificate (Unique Disability Identification (UDID) Certificate or FORM-PwD [II/III/IV, whichever is applicable]) issued by the notified medical authority.
2 Dyslexic candidates need to submit FORM-DYSLEXIC-1 and FORMDYSLEXIC-2 instead of FORM-PwD. The certificate must mention ‘SEVERE’ under Dyslexia category to enable the candidate to get the benefit of PwD category.
For foreign nationals and OCI/PIO candidates:
S No Documents Required
1 OCI/PIO Card or Foreign Passport or Citizenship Certificate

Frequently Asked Questions on JEE ADVANCED Application Form

Q- I have made a mistake in the JEE Ac4dvanced 2024 application form. Can I modify the details?

No. There is no provision to correct any mistakes made in the JEE Advanced application form.

Q- How can I reset JEE Advanced login password?

To reset the login password of JEE Advanced 2024, candidates will have to click on the forgot password option. Choose one of the options given therein and enter the asked details. A new password will be sent to the candidates.

Q- What are the login credentials to be used to fill JEE Advanced application form 2024?

Candidates have to use their JEE Main roll number and password to login into the JEE Advanced registration portal.

Q- What is the application fee for female candidates of Indian National?

The JEE Advanced 2024 application fee for female candidates of all categories is Rs. 1600.

Q- Is JEE Advanced 2024 application form released?

The authority will release the JEE Advanced application form 2024 online on April 21.

Q- When will the registration for JEE Advanced 2024 commence for Indian nationals and foreign nationals?

The registration for Indian nationals will begin on April 21, 2024, while for foreign nationals, it will commence on April 15, 2024.
