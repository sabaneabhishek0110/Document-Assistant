BITSAT Eligibility Criteria:

    Students interested in filling out the BITSAT application form 2024 have to fulfil all the eligibility criteria laid down by the authorities. Only those candidates who meet all the conditions shall be eligible to apply for BITSAT registration 2024. Check below all the conditions of BITSAT eligibility.

    > Candidates must have Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics subjects in class 12

    > Candidates should have obtained a minimum of 75% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in the class 12 examination with 60% marks in each subject.

    > Candidates must possess adequate proficiency in the English language.

    > Students who are presently studying on BITS on any campus are not eligible to apply.

Documents required for BITSAT Application Form

To fill out the application form for BITSAT 2024 hassle-free, candidates are advised to keep all documents required for BITS 2024 registration ready. This will help candidates fill BITSAT 2024 registration form quickly soon after it is released. Below is the list of documents to keep ready for the BITSAT 2024 application form.

• Class 10 and 12 marks statement. In case a candidate is currently studying in class 12, only class 10 marks statement.

• Scanned images of passport-size photographs and signature as per specifications.

• Credit/Debit card details for payment of BITSAT 2024 registration fees.

Step-wise Process on How to Fill BITSAT Application Form

Step 1- BITSAT Registration

It is the first step in applying for admission to BITS Pilani. Students have to fill the BITSAT 2024 application form by providing their name, gender, date of birth, nationality, mobile phone number, and date of birth. While filling BITS 2024 registration form, enter the details carefully, however, no changes will be allowed in registration details.

Next, candidates have to enter all the required details and click on the submit button to complete the BITSAT 2024 registration. The system will show a pop-up message with the registration number upon BITS registration 2024 successfully. Students will also receive messages on the registered email address and mobile phone number containing their Login ID and Password. It is advised to note down the BITSAT 2024 registration number, login ID, and password in a diary. For all future login, candidates will have to use this login ID and password.

Step 2: Filling Detailed BITSAT Application Form

> After successful BITS Pilani registration 2024, students will be redirected to the application form filling window.

> Next, candidates have to submit detailed information in the BITSAT application form 2024, such as address, parents, class 12 examination details, and test city choice details.

Step 3 - Uploading Scanned Photograph and Signature

> Once all details are submitted, candidates have to upload scanned images of their recent passport-size color photograph and signature. The scanned images have to be within the permissible size and format. No BITSAT application form 2024 will be accepted without the scanned images of the asked details.

Step 4 - Selecting the Preferred BITSAT Exam Centre

Here, applicants will be required to choose a minimum of three COMEDK exam centres based on their preference. The allotment of COMEDK UGET test centres will be done on the basis of the candidate’s preference. But the final decision will be made by the authorities.

Step 5 - BITSAT Application Fee Payment

Finally, to complete the application form filling of BITSAT 2024, aspirants will have to pay the BITSAT 2024 registration fee. Candidates can pay the BITSAT registration fee either online or offline. Online application fees can be paid through credit/debit card or Internet banking.

After a successful BITSAT 2024 fee payment, students get a message on their phone and email regarding their BITSAT 2024 registration fee payment. Students can also download the fee payment receipt and save it for future reference.

How to Check BITSAT Application Form 2024 Status?

Students will receive a message on their registered email address and mobile phone number about the successful submission of their BITS Pilani application form 2024. Students can also check the status of their BITSAT Application Form by logging in through their candidate portal using their login ID and password.

Frequently Asked Questions on BITSAT Application APPLICATION Form

Q- When will the BITSAT 2024 application form be released?

BITS Pilani released the application form of BITSAT 2024 on January 15.

Q- Who can apply for BITSAT 2024?

Candidates who have completed class 12 with a minimum of 75% are eligible for BITSAT.

Q- How much aggregate score I need to be eligible to apply for BITSAT 2024?

Students willing to apply for BITSAT 2024 have to obtain an aggregate score of 75% in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics/ Biology in class 12 along with 60% each in Physics, Chemistry and Biology/ Mathematics.

Q- How can I apply for BITSAT 2024?

Candidates seeking admission through BITSAT 2024 will have to visit the official website of BITS admissions - bitsadmission.com and complete the registration and form filling process. BITSAT 2024 registration fee also has to be paid.

Q-How can I check the status of my BITSAT 2024 application form?

The authorities provides an online window for checking the status of your application. Candidates have to enter their name and other details to check the status of their BITSAT 2024 registration form.

Q-Will authorities reject my application if I submit multiple BITS Pilani application forms?

Yes, the authorities will cancel the candidature of the applicants in case they fill multiple BITSAT application forms.

Q-What is BITSAT 2024 registration fee?

The BITSAT application fee will be different for different categories. For the male candidates the application fee is Rs. 3300/-; for female candidates, it will be Rs. 2800/- and for candidates (Male and Female both) opting Dubai centre the fee is US $90 (Rs. 6300).

Q- Can students make changes in the BITSAT 2024 application form after submission?

Yes, after concluding the BITSAT 2024 registration, the authorities will open the form correction window.
