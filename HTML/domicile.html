Domicile Certificate Requirements

Before applying for a Domicile Ceertificate, ensure you have the following documents:

S No Form Fields Details Required
1 Photo One passport size photograph.
2 Identity proof this could be a birth certificate, passport, driving licence, PAN Card or high school mark sheet
3 Address proof Any 1 of Passport / Water Bill / Ration Card / Aadhaar Card / Voter ID Card / Telephone Bill / Driving License / Electricity Bill / Property Tax Receipt / Extracts of 7/12 and 8 A/ Rent Receipt
4 Age proof(in case of minor) Any 1 of SFC Certificate / Birth Certificate / Bonafide Certificate / School Leaving Certificate / Extract from primary school entry
4 Other Documents (If Required) Any 1 of Water Bill,Ration Card/ Rent Receipt/ Voter List Fee/ Telephone Bill/ Electricity Bill/ Marriage certificate/ Property Tax Receipt/ Property Registration Fee/ Residence Proof of Husband/ Extracts of 7/12 and 8 A/ Rent Receipt

These documents are necessary for the verification process.

Frequently Asked Questions on Aadhar Enrollment Form

Q- What is the Time Required to get Age, Nationality and Domicile Certificate in Pune?

It will take 15 days to obtain Age, Nationality and Domicile Certificate, once the application is submitted.

Q-What is the fees of domicile certificate?

You need to pay  Rs. 33.60/- (Rs. 20 + Rs. 3.60 GST + Rs. 10 Stamp duty) for getting an Age, Nationality and Domicile Certificate in Pune.

Q- Can i get a duplicate copy?

Yes you can apply for a copy using the application number.
