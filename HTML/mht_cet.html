MHT CET Eligibility:

  • To apply for the MHT CET exam, the candidates must meet the below-listed eligibility criteria.

    > Candidates must be an Indian Citizen

    > The candidate must be a resident of Maharashtra.

    > Candidates who are not residents of Maharashtra can also apply but for a limited number of seats reserved under the All India Quota.

    > Candidates must have passed or appearing in class 12 with Physic and Mathematics along with Biology/ Chemistry/ Biotechnology/ Computer Science/ IT/ Agriculture/ Engineering Graphics/ Business Studies/ Technical Vocational subject or Passed Diploma in Engineering and Technology

    > Candidate must have secured at least 45% marks in class 12 (40% for reserved category).

Step-wise Process on How to Fill MHT CET Application Form

Step 1- MHT CET Registration

The following information has to be filled in while registering.

1. Personal Details:

• Candidate’s Full Name

• Father’s Name

• Mother’s Name

• Gender

• Date of Birth

• The region to which the candidate belongs

• Annual Family Income

• Mother Tongue

• Nationality

2. Permanent Address:

The complete address of the candidate including the Pin Code should be filled in. The mobile number and email address should be filled during registration for further communication.

3. Choose Password:

A password with a length of 8 to 13 characters must be created as per the instructions given to create a password. The next step is to click on save and proceed. After which a one-time password will be sent to the registered mobile number. The one-time password should be entered in the space given and verified. Immediately after that, an application number will be generated. The application number should be saved by the candidate.

Step 2: Filling Online Application form

The candidate must enter the application number and password generated to open the application form. The candidates must fill in the following details in the application form.

1. Domicile Details: Candidates have to select their domicile- from Maharashtra or non-Maharashtra.

2. Category Details: In this section, the candidates must fill in the following details

• Category of the candidate

• The annual income of the family

• Caste name

• Mention if the candidate is a person will disabilities

• Mention if the candidate wants to apply for an economically weaker section

3. Education Details: In this section, the candidate must fill in Class 10 and Class 12 percentages and all the related information.

4. MHT CET Examination Details: The candidates should then select the examination details. The candidates will be prompted to select the subject group, language preference and examination centre.

• Select the subject group for MHTCET: The candidates must select if it is PCM/PCB or both.

• Select the language/medium of the question paper: The candidates must select English, Marathi or Urdu.

• Select MHTCET examination centre:The candidates can enter their preferred examination centres.

After filling in all the details on the application form the candidate must click on save and proceed. The candidates can preview the details filled in on the application form and then submit it.

Step 3 - Uploading Images

Candidates also need to upload scanned images of their passport-size colour photograph and signature in the MHT CET application form. To upload the images, candidates have to click on the “Upload Photo & Signature” link at the MHT CET registration portal. The signature & photograph uploaded by the candidates must be as per the standard decided by the exam conducting authority.

For Uploading photograph

• Take your recent colour passport-size photograph. The picture should be taken against a light/white background. In case the face on the photograph is not clear, your application is liable to be rejected.

• The file size of the photograph should be between 15 KB to 50 KB otherwise, the system will not accept it.

• To reduce the size, crop the image of the passport-size photograph in MS Paint or any other Photo Editing Software.

• Please ensure that only the full face portion is cropped and not the entire photograph.

For Uploading Signature

• The candidate himself/herself should sign (no capital letters) on white paper with a black ink pen.

• Scan the signature in 200 dpi (dots per inch) scanner resolution settings and the file size should be between 5 KB to 20 KB in .jpg format.

• To reduce the size, crop the image of the signature in MS Paint or any other Photo Editor. Please ensure that only the signature portion is cropped and not the entire A4 paper on which you have signed.

• Once the photograph and signature files are uploaded by the candidate successfully, the system will show on the screen Photograph and Signature that has been uploaded. If these have been correctly uploaded, you should now move further to make the online payment.

Step 4 - MHT CET Application Fee Payment

Candidates have to pay the requisite MHT CET application fee using net banking/Credit Card/Debit Card/Mobile wallets. The service charge (as applicable) has to be borne by the candidate. Every applicant must keep in mind that the MHT CET application fee is non-refundable and non-transferable under any circumstances. Frequently Asked Questions on MHT CET Application Form

Q- Can I pay the application fee of MHT CET 2024 in offline mode?

No, candidates will be allowed to pay MHT CET 2024 application fee in online mode only.

Q- What is the last date for MHT CET registration 2024?

The last date for MHT CET 2024 registration by paying a late fee of Rs 500 was March 15.

Q- How do I make changes in the already submitted MHT CET application form 2024?

The authorities will open the MHT CET 2024 form correction facility on March 20. Candidates will be able to modify the details mentioned in the application form till March 22.

Q- Will I be required to upload the documents of academic details?

No, candidates will need the same at the time of MHT CET counselling.

Q-What are the documents need to upload at the time of filling MHT CET 2024 application form?

Candidates must upload documents including their photograph, signature, and identity proof.

Q- Are all the MHT CET exam centres located in Maharashtra state only?

No, there are some exam centres in Bihar, Chattisgarh, Delhi-NCR, Gujrat, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Telangana, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh depending upon the number of applicants.

Q- Can a student outside Maharashtra fill the MHT CET application form?

Yes, outsiders are allowed to apply for MHT CET 2024, provided that they fulfill the domicile criteria for admission to government colleges.

Q-Is MHT CET 2024 application form released?

Yes, The MHT CET 2024 application form has been released on the official website.
