Income Certificate Requirements

Before applying for a Income Ceertificate, ensure you have the following documents:

S No Form Fields Details Required
1 Photo One passport size photograph.
2 Identity proof this could be a birth certificate, passport, driving licence, PAN Card or high school mark sheet
3 Address proof Address proof- this could be either ration card, your passport, driving license or a utility bill (phone or electricity).
4 Age proof Applicant Birth Certificate /School leaving Certificate/ Pan Card.
4 Proof of income Form 16 if salaried, income tax return statement, salary slip.

These documents are necessary for the verification process.

Income Certificate Application Process

Here are the detailed steps to apply for Income Certificate card online:

⚬ Step 1:

Visit the official website of the Maharashtra Government.

⚬ Step 2:

Click on “New User” which is visible on the home page of the portal.

⚬ Step 3:

Select “Income Certificate” option from the main menu.

⚬ Step 4:

On the next page, the application form for Income Certificate appears.

⚬ Step 5:

Fill the form with required details and attach the scanned documents.

⚬ Step 6:

Finally click on “Submit” for successful registration.

Note: After submitting the application form of income certificate, you will be provided with an acknowledgement number to track your income certificate status.

How to Track Status of Income Certificate?

⚬ Step 1:

To check the status of Maharashtra income certificate application status the applicant has to revisit the official portal.

⚬ Step 2:

Click on ” Track Your Application” and enter your application ID.

Select ‘Go‘ and the status of the application saved will be displayed.

Frequently Asked Questions on Aadhar Enrollment Form

Q- What is the Eligiblity criteria?

Any individual who is employed and a resident of Maharashtra is eligible to apply for a Maharashtra income certificate.

Q- What is the use of income certificate?

Income certificate is useful for various purposes like obtaining admissions in schools, getting a loan for education, saving your income from income tax, as well as to get various other certificates like Aadhar card, ration card, domicile certificate, caste certificate, etc.

Q- What are the documents required?

  • Age Proof: Applicant Birth Certificate /School leaving Certificate/ Pan Card.
  • Address Proof: Ration Card / Voter ID /Passport/ Driving License /Electricity bill/ Water bill / property tax receipt.
  • Photograph: Applicant passport size photograph.
  • Identity Proof: Aadhar Card / Voter ID /Driving License.
  • Proof of Income: Form 16 if salaried, income tax return statement, salary slip.

Q-Is a bank statement a Proof of income

A bank statement is only supporting financial evidence but not a proof of income by itself.

Q- Who can issue a income certificate?

This will depend on the state or district, but here are some government bodies that can issue an income certificate:

  • Village Tehsildars 
  • District Magistrate
  • Sub-divisional Magistrates
  • Deputy Commissioner
  • District Collector
  • Revenue Circle Officers
  • District authorities

Q- Is income certificate and income tax certificate the same?

No, the income certificate and income tax certificate are both different. The former is a government-issued financial document that proves the annual income of the individual or the family. While the latter is the form that the company or the individual files with the government to state their annual income, tax deduction, savings, and tax owed.

Q- What is the minimum income for income certificate in India?

The minimum income required to apply for income certificate in India is equal to or less than Rs.4.50 lakh.
