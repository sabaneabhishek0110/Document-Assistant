WBJEE Eligibility Criteria:

    WBJEE Age Limit

    WBJEE eligibility criteria age limit for the candidates appearing for WBJEE is as follows.

    • As per the criteria, the candidates should be a minimum of 17 years old as of December 31, 2024.

    • Candidates should have been born on or before December 12, 2007. No upper WBJEE age limit.

    • Candidate is applying for Marine Engineering, then the candidates have to be a maximum of 25 years of age as of December 31, 2024.

    Academic Qualifications

    • Should have passed the 10+2th (or equivalent) exam conducted by a board recognized by the Central and State Governments.

    • Candidates should have qualified in Physics and Mathematics along with any one of the following Chemistry / Biology / Biotechnology / Computer Science / Computer Application as compulsory subjects in the qualifying exam.

    • The above-mentioned subjects should have also been passed individually in regular class mode (theory and practical). Candidates who will be appearing for the qualifying exam will also be considered eligible for the WBJEE 2024 exam.

    WBJEE qualifying Marks Required

    • General category candidates should have scored an aggregate of 45% in the three compulsory subjects in the 10+2th (or equivalent) examination.

    • As per WBJEE qualifying marks, reserved category candidates should have secured an aggregate of 40%.

    • Should have also qualified in English with a minimum 30% marks.

    WBJEE eligibility criteria for other state

    Candidates who have not completed their class 10 or 12 from West Bengal but have a domicile can apply for the WBJEE exam.

Documents Required for WBJEE Application form?

Candidates were advised to keep the following documents and information ready with them before filling out the WBJEE application form 2024. Please note that the information entered in the application form should correspond to the documents listed below.

• Domicile Certificate

• Category Certificate

• Annual Family Income proof

• Scholarship certificate issues from TWF (If Applicable)

• JEE Main 2024 application number (if appeared for the exam)

• Class 10th mark sheet

• Class 12th mark sheet

• Passing Certificate

• Net banking/debit card/credit card details for fee payment

A scanned passport-size photograph and signature should also be kept ready as per the specifications mentioned by the official authorities.

Step-wise Process on How to Fill WBJEE Application Form

Step 1- Online WBJEE Registration

In the first step, candidates had to complete the WBJEE registration 2024 using the given steps:.

• Visit the official website at wbjee.nic.in 2024 registration

• Click on the ‘New Registration’ link.

• Enter details like name, father’s name, mother’s name, date of birth, gender, type of identification and contact number.

• Enter other contact details (address, district, state, PIN code, email ID, mobile number, etc.)

• Create a password and security question with the answer.

• Preview the details provided during WBJEE registration and then click on ‘Submit’.

• On successful WBJEE 2024 registration, candidates will receive an application number on the registered email and mobile number.

Step 2: Filling WBJEE 2024 application form

Next, candidates had to login to the WBJEE candidate portal using the application number and password and fill all the required details of the WBJEE registration form 2024. Candidates had to choose three zones of cities to be allocated as exam centres.

Step 3 - Uploading Photograph & Signature

After filling in all the details, candidates had to upload scanned images of their photograph and signature as per the specifications provided by the authorities.3

Step 4 - Payment of Application Fee

Next, candidates need to pay the WBJEE registration form fee in online mode. Candidates have to pay Rs. 500 as WBJEE 2024 registration fees.

Frequently Asked Questions on WBJEE Application Form

Q- . Is WBJEE application form 2024 released?

Yes, the WBJEE 2024 application form was released on December 28, 2023.

Q- How can I fill the application form of WBJEE 2024?

WBJEE application form 2024 was available on the official website- wbjeeb.nic.in. Candidates had to complete the registration then fill the application form.

Q- What documents are required while filling the WBJEE 2024 application form?

During WBJEE application form 2024 submission, candidates had to upload scanned images of their photograph and signature as per the specifications provided by the authorities.

Q- How can I make corrections in the WBJEE application form 2024?

The authorities provided the WBJEE 2024 application form correction window from February 7 to 9. No corrections could be made for the Name, Father’s Name, Mother’s name, gender, domicile, and date of birth.

Q-I have not received my WBJEE 2024 application number through SMS, what should I do?

Applicants must enter a valid phone number at the time of registration and must have the mobile with them during the WBJEE application form 2024 process. The network connection must be good so that the SMS is received immediately.

Q- When WBJEE registration will start for 2024?

WBJEE 2024 registration commenced on December 28, 2023.

Q- What is the last date of WBJEE application form 2024?

The last date of WBJEE 2024 application form was February 5, 2024.
