KCET Eligibility Criteria:

    > Candidates applying for KCET must be a citizen of India.

    > Candidate must have passed 2nd PUC/12th class or equivalent exam with English as one of the languages

    > Candidate must also have scored at least 45% marks in aggregate in Physics, Maths Chemistry/BioTechnology/Biology/Electronics/Computer

    > For SC, ST, Cat-1, 2A, 2B, 3A and 3B category candidates, the minimum marks is 40% .

KCET Application Form- Flowchart

Step-wise Process on How to Fill KCET Application Form

Step 1- KCET Registration

To register for the KCET, candidates have to go to the KCET application form official website- kea.kar.nic.in and follow the given steps:

• Click on ‘New User’

• Read the given instructions and click on ‘Proceed to apply online'

• Select ‘Nationality’

• 13 digit ‘Student Number’ issued from PUE Department has to be entered by the candidates who appeared for the 12th PUC exam.

• Click on ‘Submit’

After the KCET exam registration is done, the candidates have to enter the following details KCET form:

• Candidate’s name

• Date of birth

• Email address

• Mobile number

• A password of choice has to be created

• Re-enter the password for confirmation

• Answer the security question

• Security code

• Click ‘Submit’

Candidates receive the Karnataka CET application number and User Id to their registered email ID and mobile number. It has to be kept safe since it will be used for login purposes.

Step 2: Fee Payment

The required KCET exam application fee had to be paid in online (net banking/credit card/debit card).

Step 4 - Uploading of Scanned Images

Candidates have to upload the scanned images of their photograph, signature, and left thumb impression along with their parent’s signature of left thumb impression as per the specifications mentioned by the authorities.

Step 5 - Submission of Form

• Finally, candidates have to review all of the details submitted and corrections can be done by clicking on the ‘Edit Application Form’ button. Candidates have to accept the declaration and finally submit the KCET application form.

• The printout of the KCET application form has to be attested by the School Principal or PU of the institute/school/college studied or currently studying or by any Gazetted Officer. Candidates are required to produce this attested KCET application form at the counselling session.

Frequently Asked Questions on KCET Application Form

Q- Will KCET 2024 application form be available in offline mode?

No, the KCET application form 2024 ws available only in online mode.

Q- I made a mistake while filling the CET application form. What should I do?

KEA opened the correction facility of KCET 2024 for all the registered candidates. During this period, the candidates will be able to modify and submit their KCET 2024 application form.

Q- How can I retrieve my forgotten KCET password?

Candidates can retrieve their password by clicking on the “Forget password” link and providing the required details such as KCET 2024 User ID, application number, security question, answer, and security code.

Q- What is the last date to apply for KCET 2024?

The last date to apply for KCET 2024 has been extended to April 1, up to 4 PM.

Q-I am not able to upload the scanned documents during KCET 2024 application. What should I do?

All of the documents to be uploaded in the KCET 2024 application form have to be scanned according to the specifications mentioned by the official authorities.

Q- Do I have to submit the application form of KCET 2024 in offline mode?

No. Candidates don't have to submit the KCET application form 2024 in offline mode. However, the candidates will be required to download the CET application form and get it attested by the Principal of the class 12th or PU of the institute/school/college studied or where the candidate is currently studying or by any Gazetted Officer.

Q- CHow can I pay the application fees of KCET 2024?

Candidates can pay the application fee of KCET 2024 either through online or offline mode (bank challan).

Q-What after KCET 2024 Application Form?

All of the registered candidates who fulfilled the eligibility criteria will be able to download their KCET 2024 admit card. The admit card is an important document as candidates with it are not allowed to appear for the entrance exam.
