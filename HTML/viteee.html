Steps to Fill Online VITEEE Application Form

Step 1- VITEEE Registration

Candidates will first have to register themselves for VITEEE by submitting the following details:

• Candidate’s name

• Date of birth

• Nationality

• Candidate’s gender

• Contact number (mobile number)

• Candidate’s email address

After submitting the above-mentioned details in the application form for VITEEE 2024, candidates will receive a text on their mobile and an email for confirmation which will be followed by the VITEEE login ID and password for further login.

Step 2: Filling VITEEE Application Form

Upon successful VIT registration 2024, candidates will have to log in using their email ID and password. Herein, candidates have to fill in VITEEE registration details such as personal and academic details like the name of the board of qualifying examination, year of passing, etc.

Step 3 - Payment of VIT Application Fee

In the next step, candidates have to pay the VITEEE application form fees of Rs 1350. Payment can be done in online mode through credit card/ debit card or net banking. Candidates should not forget to keep a note of the transaction ID for future reference.

Step 5 - Final VITEEE Application Form Submission

After filling in the VITEEE application form, paying the application fee and uploading the requisite documents is the necessary step. Candidates will have to finally submit the VIT application form. It is advised to download the filled-in application form of VITEEE.

International Student Applications

VIT University welcomes applications from both foreign nationals and non-resident Indians (NRIs) for admission. It is important for these candidates to carefully review the VITEEE eligibility criteria provided on the official website before proceeding with registration. Selection of candidates in this category is determined by their merit, which is assessed based on their marks or grades obtained in the qualifying examination, along with the availability of seats.

Foreign nationals and Overseas Citizens of India (OCIs) studying in any country are eligible for admission under the foreign category. Moreover, the sponsor of these candidates is required to pay the fees in US dollars. For applicants from overseas, the VITEEE application fee is USD 50. This streamlined process ensures that foreign and NRI candidates have an opportunity to pursue education at VIT University.

Frequently Asked Questions on VITEEE Application Form

Q- When will the VITEEE 2024 application form be released?

VITEEE application form 2024 has been released on November 1, 2024.

Q- How can I access VITEEE 2024 registration form?

VITEEE registration form 2024 is available in online mode. For filling the online form, candidates have to visit viteee.vit.ac.in.

Q- What are the documents required while filling the VITEEE application form 2024?

While filling the VITEEE 2024 application form, candidates have to upload scanned images of their photograph and signature as per the specifications provided by the authorities.

Q- What is VIT application form 2024 last date?

The last date for submitting the VIT application form 2024 is April 10, 2024.

Q-Is VITEEE 2024 exam online or offline?

The mode of the VITEEE exam 2024 will be online mode.
