NEET Eligibility Criteria:

    Parameters Details
    Qualifying exam for NEET 2024

    • Candidates must have passed 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English as core subjects from a recognised board.

    •Class 12 or equivalent appearing aspirants are also eligible to apply for NEET.

    • Candidates studying Biology as an additional subject after passing class 12 are also eligible.
    NEET eligibility marks 2024

    The class 12 percentage required for NEET 2024 exam is separate for each category and can be listed as under. NEET eligibility marks in 12th to be considered are aggregate marks only for PCB subjects.

    • UR - 50%

    • OBC/SC/ST - 40%

    • PWD - 40%
    Maximum Number of Attempts for NTA NEET

    There are no restrictions on the number of NEET 2024 attempt limit.

Step-wise Process on How to Fill NEET(UG) Application Form

Step 1- Registration

Candidates must visit the official new NEET UG website i.e., neet.ntaonline.in.

On the homepage, students will find the link that reads "Fill Registration Form” or the invitation for NEET UG 2024 Registration -- click on that.

Now, the candidate needs to click on 'New Registration' and read the instructions.

Now, candidates have to enter the following details in their NEET UG form:

• Candidate’s Name

• Mother’s name

• Father’s name

• Date of birth

• Category

• Gender

• Nationality

• Persons with Disability

• Identification Type

• Identification Number

• Mobile number

• Email Id

• Password

• Enter Security Pin

Step 2: Apply online for NEET

→ Candidates need to click on 'click here to login'.

→ Candidates need to log in using their application number and the given password.

→ You will be able to access the NEET application form.

→ The application will be pre-filled with the information you provide at registration; it cannot be modified.

The form is further divided into 12 sections which contains the following details :

The many NEET application form 2024 sections are listed here, along with the information that must be entered in each section. Take a look here:

• Candidate's gender

• Whether candidates suffering from diabetes

Click "Yes, please do" on the confirmation pop-up to submit the properly filled-out application form.

Step 3 - Upload scanned images

→Click on “Upload your recent documents”

→ Select the files to be uploaded

→ Click on “Next”

Step 4 - Make Payment

Choose the preferred payment method (SBI, Syndicate, HDFC, ICICI, Paytm Payment Gateway using Debit Card, Credit Card, Net-Banking, or UPI).

Frequently Asked Questions on NEET Application Form

Q- Who is the competent authority to release NEET application form 2024?

National Testing Agency (NTA) is the competent authority to release the NEET 2024 application form.

Q- Who can register and fill the NEET 2024 form?

Aspirants fulfilling the eligibility criteria can only register and fill the application form up till the NEET 2024 registration last date.

Q- Where can one register for NEET 2024?

Aspirants will be able to register themselves at the NEET 2024 registration website neet.ntaonline.in
