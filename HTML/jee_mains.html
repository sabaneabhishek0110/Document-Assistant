What are the particulars of the JEE MAINS form?

While filing the aadhaar enrollment form following details would be required to be mentioned by you. Namely,

S No Form Fields Details Required
1 Pre-Enrolment ID This would be given by the enrolment center.
2 NPR Receipt / TIN Number This field is not mandatory. But, if you already have NPR (National Population Register) receipt or survey slip, the number form same is required to be mentioned here. Alternatively, if you have a TIN (Tax Identification Number), the same can also be quoted.
3 Full Name Mention your full and complete name
4 Gender Select your gender correctly
5 Age or DOB Mention your Age as per the DOB proof submitted
6 Address Mention the address as per the Proof of address you are submitting with the application form. It would be used for all future communications and delivering a hard copy of your aadhaar card.
7 Family details Giving details of either Mother, Father, or Guardian is mandatory if an applicant is a child below 5 years. Alternatively, Husband or Wife information can also be furnished.
8 Verification Type Document Based – mention the documents you are submitting as Proof of POI, POA, DOB, POR. Introducer Based or Head of Family Based selected, in case you do not have Identity Proof or Address Proof of yourself.
9 Authentication Sign the form, giving confirmation of the particulars furnished to be true and correct.

How to fill JEE Main 2024 Application Form?

The NTA-specified steps to fill out the JEE Main Application Form 2024 are given below. Candidates who wish to know how to fill out the JEE Mains 2024 application form can refer to these easy steps.

  • Visit NTA/JEE Main website at jeemain.nta.ac.in.
  • Use log in to fill details in the application form.
  • Uploading the photo, signature and Class 12 mark sheet.
  • Verify all the details carefully.
  • Application fee payment.
  • Print out of the confirmation page.
  • Keep the confirmation page safe for reference.

JEE Main 2024 Registration Form- Important Points to Consider

  • JEE Main application form for Session 2 has commenced from February 2, 2024.
  • NTA has released the JEE Main 2024 application form online on the official website.
  • Candidates must fill the application form before the last date of registration.
  • The deadline for submitting the JEE Main online application form is March 2, 2024.
  • Students who are applying for the first time will have to complete the JEE Main 2024 registration first before filling out the application form for JEE Mains.
  • Those who do not have access to the internet can go to the nearest Common Services Centre (CSC) to fill out their JEE main application form 2024 for a nominal fee.
  • Candidates must keep their photographs and signatures ready in specified dimensions to upload to the application form. The others who appeared in the previous session can use the same ones they had used earlier.
  • To avoid technical glitches, candidates are advised to use web browsers like Internet Explorer 8 and above, Mozilla 3.0 and above, Google Chrome 3.0
  • Applicants must take printouts of the application form confirmation page for future reference.

Step-wise Process on How to Fill JEE Main Application Form 2024

Step 1- Registration

The first and foremost step is JEE Main 2024 registration. Candidates have to visit the official website- jeemain.nta.ac.in and click on "Apply for JEE Main 2024". Next, candidates will be redirected to go registration page. Candidates have to click on "New Registration".

On entering the new registration page, candidates will be shown instructions to fill out the form. Read all instructions and click on the proceed button

The registration page will open and candidates have to fill details. The following details have to be submitted in the registration form.

Personal Details

  • Candidate's Name
  • Mother's Name
  • Father's Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Identity Type (PAN card/Passport/Qualifying examination admit card with photograph/voter ID/Ration card with photograph/bank passbook with photograph/driving license/other valid Govt. identity with photograph)
  • Gender

Contact Details

  • Address
  • Locality and sub-locality
  • City/Town/Village
  • State
  • District
  • Pin Code
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Landline Number

Choose Password - At this stage, the candidates have to choose a password, security question and security answer.

Password specifications

  • The Password must have 8 to 13 characters
  • It should have at least one Upper case, at least one lower case alphabet and at least one numeric value.
  • Among these special characters - !@#$%^&*-, the password should have at least one special character.
  • To proceed, further candidates, have to click on the Next button.

After entering all details, candidates have to click on the submit button. Candidates will see the confirmation page where they need to verify all details and click on the checklist boxes. Finally, candidates have to click on "Final Submit".

Step 2- Filling JEE Main 2024 application form

In the next step, candidates have to enter the required details in the fields which include:

Personal Information

  • PwD category or not

  • Mode of Preparation

  • Place of schooling for class 10th or equivalent
  • Type of school for class 10th or equivalent
  • School board for class 10th or equivalent
  • Year of passing for class 10th or equivalent
  • Result mode for class 10th or equivalent
  • Marks details for class 10th or equivalent
  • Pass/appearing status of class 12th or equivalent
  • Name of the qualifying exam passed/appearing for class 12th or equivalent
  • Place of schooling for class 12th or equivalent
  • Type of school/college for class 12th or equivalent
  • School board for class 12th or equivalent
  • Year of passing for class 12th or equivalent
  • Result mode for class 12th or equivalent
  • Marks details for class 12th or equivalent

Additional Details

  • Are you a twin?

Parent's Income Details: Enter the following in this stage:

  • Father's qualification
  • Father occupation
  • Father annual income
  • Mother’s qualification
  • Mother’s occupation
  • Mother’s annual income
  • Guardian's qualification
  • Guardian's occupation
  • Guardian's annual income
  • Mobile Number and e-mail of the Parent or Guardian

After filling in all the details, candidates have to click on the submit button.

Frequently Asked Questions on Aadhar Enrollment Form

Q- What corrections can be made in the JEE Main application form 2024?

The following corrections are allowed in the JEE Main 2024 application form through the correction window.

  • Candidates’ Name (as per class 10th mark-sheet)
  • Mode of Examination (switch from Pen and paper-based mode to computer-based mode but not from computer-based mode to pen and paper-based mode)
  • Paper (change from Paper 1 to Paper 2 or vice versa)
  • Exam Center
  • Aadhaar Number
  • Communication Address
  • Date of Birth
  • Contact Number
  • Uploaded Signature and Photograph

Q- Can one fill up a JEE Main 2024 application form twice?

No, you can fill the JEE 2024 application form only once using the same details.

Q- Is the name under the students photograph is mandatory for JEE Main application form 2024?

Yes, It is mandatory to mention name under the photograph of the candidates.

Q- If I fill the JEE Main application early, can I get the nearby exam center?

If you fill the application form of JEE Main 2024 as soon as possible, you are most likely to get the centre of your choice.

Q-Can I edit the JEE Main 2024 application form before a fee payment?

Till the final submission, you can edit and rectify the details. However after fee payment, the form is submitted and cannot be rectified. Provided, NTA will not open the correction window after the application form submission.

Q- How can I apply for JEE Mains 2024?

Candidates need to visit the NTA JEE Main official website jeemain.nta.ac.in and click on the link to 'Apply for JEE main 2024' and proceed with the application form.

Q- Can we fill JEE Main form 2024 twice?

NTA will conduct the JEE Main 2024 in two sessions, candidates can fill the same separately for each session. However, for one particular session, JEE main application form 2024 can be filled only once.

Q-How to fill JEE Main 2024 application form?

The authority has released the JEE Main 2024 application form in online mode. The application process of JEE Main comprises of registration, form filling, uploading of signature & image, fee payment, and printing of application. Candidates can read the detailed step by step process online.
