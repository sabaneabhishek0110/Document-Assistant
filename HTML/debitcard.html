Debit Card Requirements

Before applying for a Debit Card, ensure you have the following documents:

S No Form Fields Details Required
1 Photo One passport size photograph.
2 Identity proof this could be a birth certificate, passport, driving licence, PAN Card or high school mark sheet
3 Address proof Address proof- this could be either ration card, your passport, driving license or a utility bill (phone or electricity).

These documents are necessary for the verification process.

Debit Card Application Process

Here are the detailed steps to apply for Debit card online:

⚬ Step 1:

SBI provides another hassle-free method to apply for an SBI debit card online. For this, you need to install the YONO app on your smartphone. The steps to apply for an SBI debit card via the YONO app are as follows:

⚬ Step 2:

Tap the ‘Service Request’ option.

⚬ Step 3:

Choose the ‘ATM/Debit Card’ option and enter your profile password.

⚬ Step 4:

Tap the ‘Submit’ option.

⚬ Step 5:

Tap the 'Request New/Replacement' option.

⚬ Step 6:

Choose the SBI account for which you wish to obtain a debit card.

⚬ Step 7:

Enter all the requested information including your name, card type, and address.

⚬ Step 8:

Check the box pertaining to terms and conditions and tap the ‘Next’ option.

⚬ Step 9:

An OTP will be sent to the registered mobile number you provided.

⚬ Step 10:

Step 11: Tap on ‘Submit’ after entering the OTP.

A reference number will be sent to you within some days. This reference number can be used to track your debit card.

Steps to Apply SBI Debit Card using Written Request

The customer can file a written request for a new debit card and email it to the SBI customer care email ID at customercare@sbi.co.in

How to Track Status of SbI Debit Card?

The following are the steps to track SBI Debit card delivery status online:

Step 1: Find the consignment tracking page online by visiting-www.indiapost.gov.in

Step 2: Enter the speed post consignment number

Step 3: Enter the verification code

Step 4: Click on the tab, ‘Track now’

Frequently Asked Questions on Aadhar Enrollment Form

Q- Can I apply for SBI debit card for free?

Normally, SBI debit cards are offered with your current, savings, or salary account. However, if you wish to upgrade your debit card, then you need to pay certain charges such as issuance charges or fees.

Q- Does SBI have a daily ATM withdrawal limit for debit cards?

Yes, State Bank has a daily withdrawal limit of Rs 40,000/- for Classic Debit Cards and up to Rs 1 lakh for all other SBI debit cards.

Q- What type of debit cards are accepted at State Bank ATMs?

Debit cards issued by any bank bearing Maestro, Cirrus, Visa, MasterCard and Visa Electron logos can be used at State Bank ATMs.

Q- Are the SBI debit card and ATM card the same?

No, SBI debit card and ATM card are not the same. An ATM card is a PIN-based card, used to transact in ATMs only while a Debit Card is a much more multi-functional card and is used for transacting at a lot of places like stores, restaurants, online in addition to the ATM.

Q- How much time is required after appling voter id?

After submitting the application, it takes approximately 30 days to get your Voter ID.

Q- How long does it take to get an SBI ATM card?

You can get an SBI ATM card within 7 working days.
